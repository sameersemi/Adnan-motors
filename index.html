About Us

How It Works

Have a car to sell? Send us your car's pictures and details, and we'll take care of the rest. Our team will work tirelessly to find the best buyer for your vehicle, ensuring a seamless and transparent selling experience.

Our Story

Adnan Motors has been serving the Rawalpindi community for over 10 years, providing exceptional car buying and selling experiences. Our commission-based model ensures that our customers receive competitive rates and unparalleled service.

Why Choose Us?

At Adnan Motors, we pride ourselves on our commitment to transparency, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Our team is dedicated to finding the perfect vehicle for our buyers and securing the best deals for our sellers.

Visit Us

Our office is conveniently located near Savor Food in Sadiqabad, Rawalpindi. Visit us today to explore our range of vehicles or to discuss your car selling needs. Alternatively, connect with us on WhatsApp to learn more about our services.
